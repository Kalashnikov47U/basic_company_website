WHO WE ARE

PAMPOSH GROUP of Companies is well known in leather industry for quality and consistency in sales and service. We have an organized setup spread all over India and overseas .A leading superstar trading house who has won international fame for its wide range of Quality Products and Services. Pamposh Group has a strong presence in Europe, East Asia and has started its operation in USA/Canada. The range of products include Leather, Leather products, Leather Chemicals and Horse riding equipment.

World Class Quality in manufacture, innovation in design and product development and customer service is our trademark as the Group continues to meet frequently changing user demands worldwide. Committed to providing a world-class product made with the best possible materials, on going process enhancements, quality workmanship, and on time delivery driven by an organizational commitment to superior service




























OBJECTIVE




The objective of Pamposh Group is to develop and maintain a protocol that considers the environmental compliance and performance capabilities of leather manufacturers and support sustainable and appropriate environmental business practices within the leather industry. The chemical suppliers, leather manufacturers, authorities and society work mutually to maintain the environment. We promote sustainability in the production process in all units of the company. Making use of rational resources from the environment, we seeking balance and reduction of impacts to nature and hence build the future
